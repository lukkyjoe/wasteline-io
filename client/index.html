Our Solution

The way information is currently served is broken, confusing, and too costly. Wasteline's solution is this to build the knowledge base on the disposability of every single product; one that lets you filter by the brand or manufacturer, and look up the ecological impact of that product's life cycle. And we want to harness community wisdom to build that knowledge base. Think of it as a wikipedia for waste.
